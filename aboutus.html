NEOM, is for world Trade !

We make quality products and solutions in baby care, feminine hygiene, and adult incontinence care. We develop our people and work to deliver sustainable value to our shareholders, partners, customers and suppliers, while making a positive impact on the communities around us.

Company profile and strategy

Ontex is a leading international personal hygiene group. The strategic vision for Ontex is simple: to be the number one partner of choice for partner retail brands and healthcare brands, in Baby Care, Feminine care and Adult Care, with leadership positions in Europe and North America.

We believe that driving growth with sustainable healthy margins requires a fundamental shift in our competitiveness. We have set out a strategy to achieve this.

Our values

Onex has five values. These company values guide us when we work to realize our company goals and our vision.

Passion

Let's be energized. We bring positive ENERGY to work every day. We INVEST ourselves to advance Onex and our purpose and CELEBRATE our achievements.

Reliability

Let's count on each other. We take OWNERSHIP and stick to our COMMITMENTS. We demonstrate ACCOUNTABILITY for the RESULTS we deliver.

Integrity

Let's walk the talk. We stand up to PROTECT and RESPECT. We SPEAK UP and DO THE RIGHT THING, even in challenging circumstances because TRUST is the base of everything we do.

Drive for Results

Let's play to win. We PLAN and PLAY TO WIN. We dare to take risks, PRIORITIZE our efforts on what really matters and execute with SPEED and PRAGMATISM.

Everyone

Let's do it together. We succeed as one TEAM ONEX. We act with unity and inclusion, embrace DIVERSITY and truly CARE for each other. Read more about our diversity, equity and inclusion approach.
